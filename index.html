About the Show

Where Downtown
Comes Alive

Badass Burlesque was born from the beating heart of New York City's underground arts scene. Since our first performance, we've been the city's most talked-about variety show — a raucous, glamorous, boundary-smashing celebration of the human form, wit, and daring artistry.

Each show is a hand-curated evening featuring New York's finest neo-burlesque artists, sideshow performers, comedians, and variety acts. No two nights are ever alike. Come as you are. Leave transformed.
